SHIVAM

Research Scholar at Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee working in Theoretical and Experimental Mechanics

I am working in the domains of theoretical mechanics and experimental mechanics of composite materials. My research principally concentrates on damage characterization, constitutive modeling, and the mechanical response of fiber-reinforced laminated composites subjected to finite deformation regimes. My work integrates theoretical formulations, numerical simulations, and experimental methodologies to investigate nonlinear structural behavior and complex failure mechanisms in advanced composite systems. Through my research, I aspire to contribute toward robust multiscale frameworks for predictive damage analysis and mechanics-based modeling of composite materials.
