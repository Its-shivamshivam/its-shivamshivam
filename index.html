SHIVAM

Research Scholar at Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee working in Theoretical and Experimental Mechanics

I am a research scholar at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee engaged in advanced research in the domains of theoretical mechanics and high-performance composite materials. My research principally concentrates on constitutive modeling, damage characterization, and the mechanical response of fiber-reinforced laminated composites subjected to finite deformation regimes. My work integrates theoretical formulations, computational simulations, and experimental methodologies to investigate nonlinear structural behavior and complex failure mechanisms in advanced composite systems. Through my research, I aspire to contribute toward robust multiscale frameworks for predictive damage analysis and mechanics-based modeling of composite materials.
