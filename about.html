Background & Profile

Research Scholar in Computational and Experimental Mechanics of Advanced Composite Materials.

Biography

I am SHIVAM, a Research Scholar in the Department of Civil Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee, India. I am originally from Ghaziabad and currently reside in Haridwar, India.

My research focuses on computational and experimental mechanics of advanced composite materials, particularly fiber-reinforced laminated composites subjected to large deformation loading conditions.

My work combines theoretical modeling, computational simulations, and experimental investigations to understand damage evolution, failure mechanisms, and nonlinear structural behavior in advanced composite systems.

Outside academics and research, I enjoy gardening and playing chess, which help me maintain creativity, focus, and strategic thinking.
